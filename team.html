Our Work


Our team have collated numerous datasets, performed spatial analysis and ground-truthed greenspace quality to bring you the data displayed on our web map. Whilst the focus for our investigation was the cultural and historical values of Edinburgh's greenspaces our report focused on the creation of an "Environmental Deprivation Model" (EDM) for Edinburgh. Such models are valuable for assessing how the quality and provision of services has an impact on the wellbeing of a population. However, during the course of our investiation it became clear to us that to create an EDM for Edinburgh we would require a much more comprehensive and thorough investigation. For this reason, the data we present to you now does not include our overall EDM calculations as we felt these were not truly representative of the city. Therefore, we invite you to help further our research by participating in our questionnaire.
